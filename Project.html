Avocado-- MEET YOU WORKOUT BUDDY

DESIGN OVERVIEW

Avokout Do is a fitness application aims at helping exerciser finding workout partners. , Exerciser are able to be pair with people who have similar feature and goal through online community. By increasing interaction among exerciser, users are able to supervise, encourage share experience, and even race with each other

Featurre 1

By taping the “workout” bottom at the navigation bar set on the button. The “Workout” interface enables users to either viewing the most hot exerciser activities or searching the specific exercise by means of search bar.

Feature 2

“Pair” as the main feature of “Avokout Do”, was represented as a way to link and bridge users from any place together. Based on users’ own preference, application enables to filter and classify the similar users together, and showing on the “Pair” main interface. User can tap to view the detailed information, add as friends.

Feature 3

This new training program requires support of high-level technologies that can be foreseen in the future. It is similar to face recognition that used for unlocking phone. It will just simply face to camera, and the system will capture the moment when user workout, and automatically update the information to the cloud, and compare with information that stored in the online database, It will then give instant feedback to help user correct the posture. it can greatly improve the fitness efficiency and effect for users.
